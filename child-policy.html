Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy - Sheristan

Sheristan is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and lawful environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Sheristan strictly prohibits any content, behavior, or activity that involves or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Sexualized images, text, or descriptions of minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Any attempt to share, request, or distribute CSAE material

2. User-Generated Content

All user-generated content on Sheristan is subject to moderation. We use a combination of automated systems and manual review to detect and remove content that violates our policies. Any content suspected to involve CSAE is removed immediately.

3. Reporting and Enforcement

Users who engage in or attempt to engage in CSAE-related activities will have their accounts permanently suspended or terminated without notice. Where required by law, Sheristan may report such activity to relevant law enforcement authorities.

4. User Reporting

Users are encouraged to report any content or behavior that they believe violates this policy. Reports are taken seriously and reviewed promptly.

5. Legal Compliance

Sheristan complies with all applicable laws and regulations related to child safety and online protection. We cooperate fully with lawful requests from authorities investigating CSAE-related matters.
