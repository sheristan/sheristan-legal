Privacy Policy – Sheristan

Effective Date: December 25, 2025

Sheristan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining a safe, respectful digital environment. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Sheristan mobile application (the "App").

By accessing or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Authentication Data

To create and manage your user account, we collect certain personal information when you authenticate using third-party sign-in services:

  • Google OAuth: Name, email address, and profile picture
  • Facebook OAuth: Name, email address, and profile picture

This information is used solely for authentication and profile management within the App.

1.2 Media & Storage

User profile pictures and any user-uploaded media are securely stored using Cloudinary, a third-party cloud media management service. We store only the data necessary to provide App functionality.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

When you use the App, we may automatically collect technical and usage information, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data (to improve app stability and performance)

1.4 Advertising Data

Sheristan displays advertisements through third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These services may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and interaction data to deliver relevant advertisements in accordance with their privacy policies.

2. Zero-Tolerance Policy: User Conduct & Content

We are committed to preserving the integrity of Urdu and Hindi literature and maintaining a safe, respectful community.

Users are strictly prohibited from posting, uploading, or sharing content that includes:

  • Hate Speech & Harassment: Content promoting discrimination, hatred, or violence against individuals or groups
  • NSFW & Explicit Content: Nudity, pornography, or sexually explicit or suggestive content
  • Abusive Language: Profanity, bullying, threats, or harassment
  • Misinformation & False Attribution: Fake information or incorrect attribution of literary work

Enforcement: We reserve the right to review, moderate, or remove user-generated content. Violations may result in content removal, suspension, or permanent termination of the user account without prior notice.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account management

  • Security and policy enforcement
  • Advertising and ad performance measurement

5. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures, including encryption of data in transit. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

User Rights: You may request deletion of your account and data.

How to Delete: Users may delete their account directly from the Edit Profile section of the App. Upon deletion, personal data is removed from active systems.

7. Children’s Privacy

Sheristan does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.
